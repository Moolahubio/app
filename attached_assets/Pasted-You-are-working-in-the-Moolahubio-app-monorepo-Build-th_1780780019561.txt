The mechanic (implement exactly)

A "save" = a goal allocation (transactions.type='goal_allocate') or a circle contribution (contributions row). Raw wallet deposits/withdrawals/releases/payouts do NOT count.
Per-commitment, per-cadence streaks. Each goal and each circle membership has its own streak; the period is that commitment's frequency (goal frequency weekly/biweekly/monthly; circle period = its round cadence). One qualifying save anywhere in a period keeps the flame — amount is irrelevant.
Headline = the user's longest currently-active commitment streak (the hero flame), plus lifetime best and total periods saved. Badge progress tracks that single longest continuous streak.
Freezes — 1 per 3 months, max 4/year, ≤1 used per rolling 3-month window. Auto-consumed when a period closes unsatisfied; the period shows "frozen." Never purchasable. A second miss in the same 3-month window breaks the streak even with balance.
Vacation — 1 per calendar year, up to 30 days. Pauses streak evaluation and badge-progress time (neither saves nor breaks). Refuse a second vacation in the same year. Allowance resets Jan 1 (user tz).
Badges — calendar-time based, cadence-independent. A badge is earned for every 3 months of unbroken streak (4/year). A daily, weekly, and monthly saver earn the same badge for the same 3 months (measure elapsed time from the streak's startedAt, NOT period count). Each calendar year has a unique, collectible badge set. Earned badges persist forever on the profile even if the streak later breaks; a break resets progress toward the next badge only.
Reminder — at most one opt-in nudge per period, before the period closes, only if enabled and unsaved, respecting quiet hours. Mirror the "Streak Alert" toggle.
Rewards are cosmetic only (badges/tiers). No fee discount, yield, or cash in v1.
Timezone: evaluate periods, badges, freezes, and vacation in the user's local timezone (store timezone on the user; default UTC).

2. Backend (artifacts/api-server)

lib/streaks.ts:

recordSave(userId, commitment, txId) — call after the ledger commit in lib/goals.ts allocateToGoal() and lib/circles.ts contribute(). Marks the current period satisfied; increments the streak if it's the period's first qualifying save; idempotent.
evaluateStreaks() — daily job: for each streak past currentPeriodEnd (user tz), roll forward if satisfied; else consume a freeze (respecting the rolling-window limit); else if on vacation, stay paused; else break (reset to 0, record bestCount). Award the next quarterly badge when now − startedAt crosses a 3-month boundary while unbroken (tag year,quarterIndex). Grant freezes (1/quarter, cap 4/yr). Enqueue ≤1 reminder per (streak, period) for opted-in users via the existing notify(). Idempotent via streak_periods.
startStreakLoop() in index.ts (interval like the settlement loop) + recover on boot.
